Dataset

LFW Face

Labeled Faces in the Wild is a public benchmark for face verification, also known as pair matching. No matter what the performance of an algorithm on LFW, it should not be used to conclude that an algorithm is suitable for any commercial purpose [Data]

CelebA Database

Labeled Faces in the Wild is a public benchmark for face verification, also known as pair matching. No matter what the performance of an algorithm on LFW, it should not be used to conclude that an algorithm is suitable for any commercial purpose [Data]

Tufts Face Database

With over 100,000 images, the Tufts Face Database includes a huge collection of facial images divided into nine categories. The categories include computerized sketches, thermal, thermal cropped, three dimensional, Lytro, 2D RGB around, 2D RGB emotion, night vision, and video.[Data]

UMDFaces

UMDFaces dataset has over 367,000 face annotations across over 8,200 different subjects in still images. Apart from those images, the dataset also includes over 3.7 million video frames all annotated with facial keypoints of over 3,100 subjects. [Data]

UTKFace

The UTKFace dataset includes faces from a wide age range. The people in these images range from less than a year old to over 100 years old. The dataset includes over 20,000 face images with age, gender, and ethnicity annotations. [Data]

YouTube Faces with Facial Keypoints

This dataset is composed of public Youtube videos of celebrities which total 155,560 still frames. The videos have been cropped around the faces of the celebrities and have been annotated with facial keypoints for each frame of every video. [Data]
